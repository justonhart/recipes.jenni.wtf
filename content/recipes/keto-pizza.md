## Ingredients

- Crust ingredients: (about 15 carbs per pizza)
    - 1/2 cup almond flour
    - 1 can of cooked chicken (12-13 ounce)
    - 2 eggs
    - 4 oz shredded mozzarella
    - 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp oregano
- Toppings:
    - Pizza sauce
    - 8 oz shredded mozzarella
    - Pepperoni

## Preparation

1. First drain the chicken and pat it down with paper towel, hand shred it very fine or use a fork
2. Combine chicken, spices, eggs, almond flour, and 4 oz finely shredded mozzarella cheese in a mixing bowl
3. Mix it into a nice gooey ball, take the ball and spread it evenly across a pizza pan
4. Next bake the crust for 15-18 minutes at 500 degrees on center rack. Make sure the crust is ultra crispy on top because the bottom is less so.
5. Remove and preheat oven to broil
6. Add pizza sauce, one 8 oz bag of mozzarella cheese and toppings of choice
7. Broil until cheese is bubbly golden brown (about 5 minutes)
8. Let rest for 5-10 minutes, sprinkle the cheese with garlic powder
