## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded mozzarella
- 2 ounces cream cheese
- 1 large egg
- ¾ cup almond flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 500°F and line a pizza pan or baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Melt the mozzarella and cream cheese together until smooth, then stir in the egg, almond flour, baking powder, garlic powder, and salt until a gooey dough forms.
3. Press the dough evenly onto the prepared pan.
4. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes, until the crust is browned and crisp on top.
5. Add pizza sauce, cheese, and toppings, then bake again until the toppings are hot and melted.
