## Ingredients

- 1 cup coconut oil
- ½ cup natural peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon liquid stevia
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 ounce chopped roasted salted peanuts

## Preparation

1. Melt the coconut oil in a saucepan over low heat, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in the peanut butter until smooth.
2. Whisk in the heavy cream, cocoa powder, liquid stevia, vanilla extract, and salt.
3. Pour the mixture into 12 silicone muffin molds. Sprinkle the peanuts evenly over the top and place the molds on a baking sheet.
4. Freeze until firm, at least 1 hour. Unmold the cups and store them in a resealable plastic bag or airtight container.
