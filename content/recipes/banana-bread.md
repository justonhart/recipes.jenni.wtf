## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter (room temp)
- 2 eggs
- 3 bananas (extra ripe)
- 3 tbsp buttermilk
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 cups flour
- a shake of salt

## Preparation
1. Mix all ingredients, pour into greased loaf pan
2. Bake at 325 for an hour or until toothpick comes out clean 

## Notes
- If baking in muffin pan, bake for 20 minutes
